Our story

Curry Madras, a culinary gem nestled in the heart of Tournai, Belgium, embarked on its extraordinary gastronomic journey several decades ago. Founded by the passionate and visionary chef Cl√©ment Lequenne, Curry Madras quickly became a sought-after destination for aficionados of authentic Indian cuisine. Specializing in tandoori cooking, where each dish is prepared with exotic spices and cooked in a traditional clay oven, Curry Madras has delighted palates with unique flavors and warm hospitality.
Over the years, the success of Curry Madras has been such that it expanded its culinary influence far beyond Tournai. Today, Curry Madras is a household name throughout Wallonia and Brussels. Locals and visitors flock to its restaurants, drawn by the promise of delights such as chicken tikka masala, crispy samosas, and fragrant curry dishes. This culinary saga has successfully blended tradition and innovation, making Curry Madras a true institution of Indian cuisine in Belgium, and a reference for those seeking to explore the captivating flavors of India.
